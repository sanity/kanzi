2.3. Registration

Initially when you install Kanzi it will be in evaluation mode, this will be indicated by the words "Evaluation Version" at the bottom of the Kanzi window. This is designed to allow you to determine whether Kanzi is useful to you before purchasing it, but it cannot be used with a real website as it randomly replaces text with a warning message in any web pages that it modifies.

To use Kanzi properly, you must obtain a license key. This may be purchased from http://cematics.com/site.php/kanzi.

After purchasing the license key, it will be emailed to the address you provided. To register, follow these simple steps:

  • Launch Kanzi

  • Click on the "Register Now" button

  • Copy the license key from the email (Ctrl+C) including the lines containing the words "BEGIN" and "END".

  • Click on "OK"

If you have any problems with this, please contact Cematics Support at support@cematics.com, please include the email address that you used to register Kanzi.
