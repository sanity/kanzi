Chapter 4. Troubleshooting

4.1. Kanzi applied my changes incorrectly

Kanzi is smart, but it does have limitations and may occasionally apply your changes incorrectly. If this happens, try splitting up the changes, first applying one set, then applying the other. The fewer changes you make to the example document - the easier it is for Kanzi.

If this doesn't work, you should email the target document (before and after your edit), and one of the documents to which the change was misapplied (before any modification) to support@cematics.com for us to take a look. We will do our best to determine why Kanzi failed and will send you the next revision of Kanzi when it is ready.
